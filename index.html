Joy's All Time Best Movies

Top 3 movies

Mouse

This is my favourite K-drama. The suspense was insane. It was everything I did not expect it to be. Plus I emotionally connected to the lead character.

Ginny and Georgia

This is my favourite American Series. The acting was top notch. I connected to the series in every way. My favourite character is Georgia.

Breath of Life

This is my favourite Nollywood movie. The story was well told plus I almost cried in a scene.
